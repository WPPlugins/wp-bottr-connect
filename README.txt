Chat bot is best live chat tool for your wordpress website or blog. Bot can automate your FAQs, improve user engagement and customer service.

== Description ==

Chat bot by Bottr is a complete WordPress plugin that allows you to add your widget a.k.a. your personal chat bot to your website or blog in a jiffy.
 
A bot is your smart digital twin - it learns by simply talking to you, represents you to the world 24/7 and brings everything in one interface!

= Why chatbot is best live chat tool? =
**Save time:** Your bot automagically learns about you from your site, blog, social feeds etc. and responds to messages on your behalf in an interactive way anywhere on the web. With your universal identity replace boring and slow contact forms, DMs, emails etc.
 
**Improve engagement:** Create engaging micro articles and blogs for your audience and share with the world in a click. Your audience can know you better and discover content by simply chatting with your bot
 
**Sell more:** Showcase your portfolio, services or merchandise in a beautiful interactive form through a chat bot

= What are the features? =

1. **Do what you love while your smart digital twin works in the background 24/7**
Your bot uses advanced artificial intelligence to learn about you and responds to your messages in an interactive way
 
2. **Get known better by showcasing what matters to you**
Train your bot for any purpose - profile, FAQs, blog, storefront etc. - by simply chatting with it. Use rich content cards to reflect your personality and character
 
3. **Your one universal inbox that works everywhere**
Share your personalized button or link anywhere - email, website, blog, social profile etc. - and let anyone have a conversation with your bot anytime 

4. **Remove clutter with your virtual home on the web**
All your info from different platforms - social networks, website, blog etc. - smartly presented in a cool conversational format with just a click
 
5. **Stay in control with live insights and notifications**
Read unanswered messages and detailed analytics to know how people are interacting with your bot. Reply directly in the same conversation
 
If you thought embedding your bot required mandatory HTML skills and copy pasting code snippets to your site's underlying code - think again! With the Bottr Chatbot plugin , all you need to do is sign in with your existing bottr id and your bot widget will be automatically embedded on your site.
 
Capturing site visitors with engaging conversations, content suggestions and a bit of personality has never been easier.  

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Navigate to `Bottr Chat bot` from WordPress admin dashboard
1. [Signup](https://bottr.me?auth=true&utm_source=WordPress_Plugin_ReadMe "Bottr: Create Chat Bot. For Influencers, Bloggers and Celebs") on bottr and create your bot
1. Come back and add your bot url
